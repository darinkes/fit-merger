Frequently asked questions

Are my files uploaded anywhere?

No. fitmerge runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly, so your GPX and FIT files never leave your device.

How do I merge GPX, FIT or TCX files?

Drop two or more .gpx, .fit or .tcx files onto the page, choose GPX, FIT or TCX output, then click Merge activities to download the combined file.

Can I mix GPX, FIT and TCX files?

Yes. You can combine any mix of GPX, FIT and TCX inputs and export the result as GPX, FIT or TCX.

Can I convert one file to another format (FIT to GPX, GPX to FIT, …)?

Yes. Switch to Convert mode, drop a single .gpx, .fit or .tcx file, pick the output format, and download the converted file — entirely in your browser.

Does it recompute distance, elevation and time?

Yes. Distance, ascent and descent, moving and elapsed time, and average and maximum speed are all recomputed from the merged points — never copied from the source files — so the totals match the combined track.

Will the merged file work in Strava and Garmin Connect?

Yes. FIT output carries correct session and lap summaries, so the totals import correctly into Strava, Garmin Connect and similar tools.
